# Product Overview

**GS Produções Intranet** is an internal portal for centralizing corporate communication, forms, and policies.

## Purpose
- Centralize internal workflows and documentation
- Provide corporate forms (overtime requests, expense reports)
- Host company policies and institutional content
- Serve as a modern, accessible intranet platform

## Key Areas
- **Public area**: Institutional presentation and hero sections
- **Internal area**: Corporate forms, policy hub, and internal tools
- **Workspace features**: Business opportunities pipeline, commercial proposals, equipment management, event scheduling

## Target Users
Internal employees and staff of GS Produções requiring access to corporate resources and workflows.
