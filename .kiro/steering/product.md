# Product Overview

GS Produções Intranet is an internal corporate portal that serves as a centralized hub for communication, corporate forms, and internal policies. The platform is evolving into a comprehensive SaaS solution for commercial teams, project operations, and executive boards.

## Core Modules

**Current Features:**
- Public institutional area with hero sections and brand presentation
- Internal area with corporate forms (overtime requests, expense reports)
- Corporate policies hub
- Dark/light mode with responsive navigation

**In Development - GS Propostas (Commercial Platform):**
- Opportunity Dashboard with Kanban pipeline (Open, In Progress, Won, Lost)
- Commercial Proposals with dynamic PDF preview and tabbed editor
- Activity tracking with calendar integration
- BI Reports and dashboards (revenue, items, activities)
- Master data management (clients, companies, items, users)

## Target Users

- Internal employees (forms and policies access)
- Sales teams (opportunity management)
- Project managers (activity tracking)
- Executives (BI dashboards and reports)

## Key Characteristics

- Modern, accessible interface with consistent UX
- Component-based architecture for reusability
- Real-time updates and collaborative features
- Multi-company support with role-based permissions
